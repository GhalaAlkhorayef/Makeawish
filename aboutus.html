Making Dreams Come True Together

Support a Campaign

Make A Wish was launched in 2023 with a simple mission: to bring hope, happiness, and lasting change to communities across Saudi Arabia. Through meaningful campaigns, we help turn dreams into reality by providing clean water, education, healthcare, and essential support to families in need.

Together with our donors and volunteers, we are building a future where compassion drives impact. By uniting hearts and efforts, we empower communities, inspire change, and make a lasting difference—one wish at a time. Join us as we continue to spread hope and create a better tomorrow for all.

Our Mission

At Make A Wish, our mission is to inspire hope, spark change, and transform lives across Saudi Arabia. We believe that every dream deserves the chance to grow into reality — and through collective kindness, we make it happen.

Our Values

Compassion

We lead with empathy and kindness at our core.

Transparency

Clear, honest communication for every donation.

Community

Building stronger futures together, one step at a time.

Why Make A Wish?

Every act of giving through Make A Wish fuels real hope. Your support doesn’t just fund projects — it lifts hearts, empowers families, and strengthens entire communities.

We are committed to transparency, trust, and building a brighter future together — one wish at a time.
